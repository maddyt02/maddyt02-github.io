About Me

My name is Madison, and I am an undergraduate student at Northeastern University in my third year. I major in Business Administration with a concentration in Finance, and I minor in Data Science. I am originally from Short Hills, New Jersey, but I now live in Boston while attending Northeastern. Living in Boston has been an exciting experience, and I have enjoyed exploring the city while meeting people from many different backgrounds.

Outside of academics, I enjoy staying active and spending time doing things that help me relax and recharge. One of my favorite activities is skiing, which has become one of my main hobbies. Growing up, my primary sport was figure skating, and I spent many years practicing and competing. Although I no longer skate as often, that experience taught me discipline, perseverance, and how to work toward long-term goals.

I also love dogs, and my family has three of them named Rufus, Muttly, and Mochi. Spending time with my friends, family, and pets is something I value a lot. I also enjoy listening to music and having new experiences.

Traveling is very important to me because it allows me to experience different cultures and perspectives. In 2026, I visited Amsterdam and Guatemala, and later this year I plan to travel to Canada and Brazil. I will also be studying abroad in Madrid this fall, which I am incredibly excited about. Academically and professionally, I am interested in pursuing opportunities in finance where I can combine analytical thinking with data-driven decision making.
