About Me

My name is Madison, and I am an undergraduate student at Northeastern University in my third year. I major in Business Administration with a concentration in Finance, and I minor in Data Science. currently a student interested in learning more about technology, business, and creative problem-solving. I created this website as a practice assignment to learn the basics of HTML, CSS, and how websites are published using GitHub Pages. While this is a simple project, it is helping me understand how web pages are structured and how multiple pages can connect through navigation links.

One thing that makes this assignment interesting to me is that it combines creativity with technical skills. I enjoy opportunities where I can organize information clearly while also making something visually appealing. In school and in other projects, I often like finding ways to communicate ideas in a way that is both useful and engaging. Building a personal website is a good example of that because it allows me to share information about myself while also practicing coding skills that can be valuable in many different fields.

Outside of academics, I enjoy spending time with friends and family, exploring new experiences, and working toward personal and professional goals. I also appreciate projects that give me the chance to learn by doing, because I usually understand things best when I can apply them directly. This website is one small example of that process. As I continue improving it, I hope to make it more personalized, polished, and reflective of my interests. For now, my goal is to create a clear, organized, and complete website that satisfies the assignment requirements and gives me a strong introduction to web design.
